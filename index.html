About

Built for the businesses that build your community.

I'm Finn Formato — the founder of Focal Media, a web design company built specifically for local businesses. I started Focal Media because I saw a gap: too many great local businesses were losing customers online simply because their websites didn't reflect the quality of their work.

I work with auto shops, detailing services, contractors, home service companies, and other local businesses that need a stronger online presence. My focus is building clean, fast, mobile-friendly websites that help businesses look more professional, rank better on Google, and convert more visitors into paying customers.

If your business deserves a better website, let's make it happen.
